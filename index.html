How GIS Mapping Services in Punjab Are Transforming Urban Planning

Urban planning in Punjab is undergoing a significant transformation, thanks to GIS (Geographic Information System) mapping services. This technology is revolutionizing the way cities are planned, developed, and managed by providing accurate and data-driven insights. What is GIS Mapping? GIS mapping is a technology that captures, stores, analyzes, and presents geographical data. It enables planners to visualize spatial relationships, identify patterns, and make informed decisions about infrastructure, transportation, land use, and environmental sustainability.
